Cash365

Personal expense tracking, synced to your own Google account.

About

Cash365 is an Android app to track your personal income and expenses. Your data can optionally be synced to your own Google account (Google Sheets and Google Drive), so it stays under your control.

Features

  • Record income and expenses with categories
  • Review balances and trends over time
  • Optional sync to your own Google Sheets and Drive
  • Works fully offline, without an account
