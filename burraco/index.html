Burraco Counter

Score counter for the card game Burraco, fully offline.

About

Burraco Counter is an Android app to keep score while playing Burraco. It handles the full scoring for you and keeps a history of your matches and player statistics. It works entirely offline, with no account and no ads.

Features

  • Automatic Burraco scoring for each hand
  • Play head to head or in teams, including the 6-player mode (two teams of three)
  • Match history and per-player statistics
  • Light and dark theme, with language switcher
  • Works fully offline, without an account
