Privacy Policy for Burraco Counter

Last updated: June 29th 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Burraco Counter. Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use Burraco Counter.

Information Collection and Use

Burraco Counter does not collect any personal information about you, and the developer does not receive, store, or have access to any of your data. The app works fully offline, without any account and without any network connection.

Everything you enter, such as player names, match scores, and statistics, is stored only locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not synced to any server, and is not shared with the developer or any third party. Uninstalling the app removes all of its data from your device.

Log Data and Telemetry

Burraco Counter does not collect any log data, analytics, or telemetry, and operates no servers. No usage information is sent to the developer or to any third party.

Cookies

Burraco Counter does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.

Third Party Services

Burraco Counter does not communicate with any server and does not use any third-party service, advertising network, or analytics SDK that collects or shares your personal information.

Security

Because all of your data stays on your own device, it is protected by your device's own security. We take reasonable measures to help protect information from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is ever fully secure.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use and protect your information.
