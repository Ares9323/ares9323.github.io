Filament Manager

Track and manage your 3D printing filament spools.

About

Filament Manager is an Android app to keep track of your 3D printing filaments: remaining weight, spools, usage logs, costs and more. Your data can optionally be synced to your own Google account (Google Sheets and Google Drive), so it stays under your control.

Features

  • Catalog your filament spools with brand, material, color and cost
  • Log usage and track remaining weight
  • QR / barcode scanning for quick spool identification
  • Optional sync to your own Google Sheets and Drive
  • Works fully offline, without an account

Get it on Google Play

Privacy

Filament Manager only accesses files it creates in your Google Drive and the spreadsheet it manages for you. Read the full Privacy Policy.
