About Me

Hi! I'm Ares, a game developer from Pesaro, Italy. I work in Unreal Engine every day as a programmer and tech artist, and over the years I've collaborated with studios in Italy and abroad on everything from procedural generation and enemy AI to VR experiences and virtual production.

I also enjoy game jams, with a few awards along the way, and I build and sell my own tools and plugins for Unreal Engine on FAB.
